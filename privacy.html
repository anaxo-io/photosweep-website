Privacy Policy – Photo Sweep

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Photo Sweep is developed and published by Anaxo Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information and privacy.

1. Overview

Photo Sweep is a privacy-first photo cleaner that helps you find and remove temporary photos — such as screenshots, receipts, and notes — entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data or photos.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Photo Sweep does not:

  • Upload or transmit your photos to any server
  • Collect personal information (such as name, email, or contacts)
  • Track your activity or use analytics tools

All processing (photo scanning, classification, and deletion) happens locally on your device’s storage.

3. Permissions

To function properly, Photo Sweep requests the following permissions:

  • Access Photos and Media: Required to scan and display photos stored on your device. This access never leaves your phone.
  • Delete or Trash Media: Required to remove selected photos using Android’s secure delete interface.

These permissions are used only to provide the app’s core functionality and are never used for analytics or tracking.

4. Data Security

Because all analysis occurs on-device, no data is sent to external servers. Your photos remain fully under your control and subject to Android's security and privacy protections.

5. Third-Party Advertising

Photo Sweep uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect a limited set of device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) to provide and personalize ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Photo Sweep does not target or knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

7. Policy Updates

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. All versions will remain available at https://anaxo.io/privacy.html.
